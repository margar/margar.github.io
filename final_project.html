About Me: Maria

Hi, my name is Maria Garcia. I grew up in The Woodlands, TX but now live in Mountain View, CA. I graduated from Texas A&M (WHOOP!) in August 2012 with a Bachelor of Science degree in Sociology and moved out to the Bay Area shortly after. Originally I thought I would work for a non-profit organization but somehow I ended up working at Groupon as an office manager. Besides making sure everything runs smoothly at the Palo Alto offices, I also run the Palo Alto Employee Volunteer Program - we organize fun opportunities to help those less fortunate and work with some amazing non-profit organizations. I love being able to interact with lots of great people at my job and creating a fun company culture! In my spare time I like to volunteer, be outside (California has the perfect weather for that!), and spend quality time with Netflix! My newest challenge has been learning to code! This is my first big project! :)
